Accessibility or a11y is

For abled people, it can be hard to empathize with disabled people because it is not how we experience the world.

To better understand why we should design and code for accessibility, let's empathize with our users.

Before learning how to build accessible web applications, learn how to empathize.

To start, let's take a look at some tools to help us.
